Common HTML Entities

The following are reserved characters in HTML:

  • Less Than: <
  • Greater Than: >
  • Ampersand: &
  • Double Quote: "
  • Single Quote: '

Some common mathematical symbols:

  • Plus-Minus: ±
  • Multiplication: ×
  • Division: ÷
  • Greater Than or Equal To: ≥
  • Less Than or Equal To: ≤
  • Infinity: ∞

Currency symbols used worldwide:

  • Dollar: $
  • Euro: €
  • Pound: £
  • Yen: ¥
  • Indian Rupee: ₹

Miscellaneous symbols:

  • Trademark: ™
  • Registered: ®
  • Copyright: ©
  • Section: §
  • Degree: °
  • Bullet Point: •
  • Middle Dot: ·

Some Greek letters:

  • Alpha: α
  • Beta: β
  • Gamma: γ
  • Delta: δ
  • Pi: π
  • Omega: ω
